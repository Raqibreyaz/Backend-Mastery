## 06. CORS (10 questions)

1. Explain the complete CORS flow from browser to server, including preflight requests and when they occur.
2. How do you configure the `cors` middleware in Express for different security levels (development vs. production)?
3. What are the differences between `origin`, `methods`, `headers`, and `credentials` in CORS configuration, and how do they affect security?
4. How do you handle CORS for multiple origins dynamically, and what are the security implications of each approach?
5. Explain the difference between simple requests and preflight requests in CORS. What triggers a preflight?
6. How do you configure CORS for specific routes only, rather than globally across your entire application?
7. What is the purpose of `Access-Control-Allow-Origin`, `Access-Control-Allow-Methods`, and `Access-Control-Allow-Headers`, and how do they interact?
8. How do you handle CORS with authentication tokens, and what are the security considerations for `Access-Control-Allow-Credentials`?
9. Explain how to implement custom CORS middleware in Express without using the `cors` package, and what edge cases you need to handle.
10. What are common CORS errors in production, and how do you debug and fix them effectively?
