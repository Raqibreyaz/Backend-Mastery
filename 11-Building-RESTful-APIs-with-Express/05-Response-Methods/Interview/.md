## 05. Response-Methods (10 questions)

1. Explain the complete set of `res` methods in Express (`res.send()`, `res.json()`, `res.sendFile()`, `res.download()`, `res.redirect()`, etc.) and their differences.
2. How does `res.send()` automatically determine the response content type, and when should you use it over `res.json()`?
3. What is the difference between `res.sendFile()` and `res.download()`, and when would you choose one over the other?
4. How do you set multiple HTTP headers efficiently using Express response methods, and what are the performance implications?
5. Explain how `res.status()` and `res.set()` work together, and what happens if you call them in different orders.
6. What is the purpose of `res.locals`, and how does it differ from other response properties in terms of scope and lifetime?
7. How do you implement streaming responses in Express, and what are the benefits over traditional response methods?
8. Explain the difference between `res.end()` and returning from a middleware function. When would you use each?
9. How does Express handle response buffering, and when should you disable it for large responses?
10. What are common mistakes when using response methods, and how do you avoid double-response errors?
