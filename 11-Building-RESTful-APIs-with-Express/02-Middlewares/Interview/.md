## 02. Middlewares (10 questions)

1. What is the complete execution flow of Express middleware, and how does `next()` control this flow?
2. Differentiate between application-level middleware, router-level middleware, and built-in middleware. Provide examples of each.
3. How do you create error-handling middleware, and what makes it different from regular middleware (including the 4-parameter signature)?
4. Explain the concept of middleware ordering. What happens if you define middleware after a route that matches the request?
5. How can you conditionally skip middleware execution without calling `next()`? What are the best practices?
6. What is the difference between synchronous and asynchronous middleware, and how does Express handle errors in async middleware?
7. How do you create middleware that can be reused across multiple routes or applications? What patterns work best?
8. Explain middleware composition. How can you chain multiple middleware functions together effectively?
9. What are common pitfalls when using middleware with Express, and how do you avoid memory leaks or infinite loops?
10. How does third-party middleware (like `cors`, `body-parser`, `express-session`) integrate with Express's internal middleware system?
